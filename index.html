Pick 'imp' Mix is a project that my dad and i completed over the summer for me to practice drawing retro style pixel characters based on my enjoyment of playing 8-bit and 16-bit games and for my dad to learn more about blockchains. we started with a set of 512 unique imps all with differing rarities based on their randomly selected attributes.

Cinematic Icons

As part of the launch of the set we created 8 one-off imps that represented some of our favourite movie characters.
